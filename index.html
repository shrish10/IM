FAQ

Got a question? We're here to answer !

If you don't see your question here drop a message on our contact page

Where does your honey come from?

Our honey is organically sourced from regions across New Zealand. To know more about where our honey comes from click here.

What is special about your honey?

Our Honey is known for its premium quality and is organically made.

Where and How long can the honey be stored?

We recommend storing our Honey in a glass container in a cool and dry place.

Why is your honey priced slightly higher?

Hive Minds Honey is priced a little higher than the other honey available in the market because it is pure organic honey and every extra NZ$ 4 charged goes directly to the Beekeeper which they actually deserve.

Where can I know more about the beekeepers who produced this honey?

We have a special section on our website dedicated to our Beekeepers stories. Also, every bottle of honey has a tag attached to it with a QR code. You can scan it to know more about the particular Beekeeper who made that bottle of honey and which region it is from.

How can I hear about new products and offers?

You can sign up through our website for regular updates on our product, recipe ideas, beekeeper stories and more! Click here to Sign up.
