Our Work


About their Work:

We follow a strict set of standardized rules while beekeeping and extraction of honey.


  • Proper training for beekeepers.
  • Bee Banks - extra bee colonies in production.
  • Protect the natural health of bees.
  • Avoid toxic pesticides and synthetic treatments instead essential oil (natural products) should be used.
  • Avoid relocation of hives. If necessary shall take place after permission from the certification agent.
  • Hives should be built using wood -recyclable natural occurring material
  • Apiaries should be placed at a distance at least 1.5metres from each other so as to avoid transfer of diseases.
  • Apiaries should be surrounded with nectar and pollen rich plants.
  • The bees should be fed their own honey instead of synthetic materials. Reserves of honey and pollination should be left in the hives.
  • Inventory should be maintained of all the colonies.
  • Extraction of Honey - the vessels and equipment used in extraction of honey should be appropriate as per the food processing standards.
  • Storage and Conservation - Honey should be stored in cool, dry place and in glass containers.
  • Documentation of the process of honey extraction and removal of supers should take place.
  • Organic Certification and testing should be done like UMF grading and Apiary Certification by Bio Gro.

OUR MOTTO:

Training and Empowering Individual Beekeepers

By creating a national database of local individual beekeepers to help them survive in this fiercely competitive market. Creating a distribution chain to help small beekeepers by managing supply and demand and helping them on a production level too, to make sure enough honey so supply equals the demand.


Promoting Sustainable Beekeeping

Our methods of Beekeeping, Honey production, Storage of honey are 100% sustainable. We take utmost care in protecting the health and wellbeing of the bees and our surrounding environment. We know that continuous Sustainable Beekeeping will help preserve our mother nature and hence, we follow a transparent journey so that you can see how we work.


Organic Honey

We produce excellent quality organic honey and make sure to follow all the measures to maintain its quality. We want our customers to be able to consume premium quality organic honey. Hence, our honey is unadulterated and is made without chemical additives and preservatives. We make sure to check the quality, taste, colour and aroma of our honey.
