About UTMT

Under The Mango Tree Society works with small individual beekeepers from across New Zealand on beekeeping sustainably. Our focus is on Sustainable Beekeeping, Protect the health and wellbeing of the bees and Empowerment of Beekeepers.

About our Local Partner - Apiculture New Zealand

Apiculture New Zealand works for all sectors of the apiculture industry from backyard beekeepers, to commercials and honey exporters. We support, advocate for and deliver benefits to the apiculture industry. Apiculture New Zealand Organization helps UTMT Society with its vast database of beekeepers.
